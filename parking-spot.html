Parking Spot Application - Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 1, 2023

This privacy policy ("Policy") explains how ["Parking Spot"] (referred to as "Application" or "We") collects, uses, and discloses information about users of the mobile application. The Application allows users to record and manage their vehicle parking spots. Please read this Policy carefully to understand our practices. By using the Application, you consent to the terms of this Policy.

1. Collected Information

The Application only uses GPS or other location data to accurately record vehicle parking spots. We do not collect, store, or request personal account information from our users.

2. Google AdMob Ads

The Parking Spot Application may use Google AdMob ads to support and offer it for free. Google AdMob may collect information such as your device's advertising identifier, location data, or other data for ad targeting purposes to deliver more relevant and engaging ads. This information may be used to customize the content of the ads based on your interests.

Ads Suitable for Children

The ads displayed in the Parking Spot Application will not be targeted for children. Ad contents obtained from ad providers are selected and delivered in a manner that protects children's privacy and safety, aiming to provide a safe experience for child users.

Ad Categories

To ensure user safety and privacy, specific ad categories containing inappropriate content for children will not be shown or presented within the Application.

3. Security

The Application takes appropriate technical and administrative measures to protect user information. However, please note that no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security. Users are also advised to use a strong and unique password to protect their personal information.

4. Children's Privacy

The Application does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will promptly delete such information.

5. Other Links

Our Application may contain links to other websites or applications. However, we are not responsible for the information sharing practices or contents of these linked sites. We recommend being cautious and reading the privacy policies of visited websites.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Policy may be updated from time to time due to changes in Application functionality or legal requirements. We will use appropriate means to notify you of these changes. Please check our Policy regularly for updates.
